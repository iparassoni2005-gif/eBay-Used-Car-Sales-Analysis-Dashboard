## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an **interactive Power BI dashboard** built to analyze **eBay used car sales data**. It provides deep insights into car pricing trends, brand performance, and market demand — helping buyers and sellers make smarter decisions in the used car market.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze **1,000+ eBay used car listings** across multiple brands
- Track **average selling price** by brand, year, and condition
- Identify **top-selling car models** and price ranges
- Monitor **Year-over-Year (YoY) pricing trends**
- Enable dynamic filtering by brand, fuel type, transmission, and condition

---

| **Power Query** | Data cleaning & transformation |

---

## 📈 Key Features

- ✅ **Brand-wise Price Analysis** — Compare average prices across car brands
- ✅ **Year & Condition Filters** — Dynamic slicers for year, condition, and fuel type
- ✅ **Top 10 Models** — Most listed and highest-priced car models
- ✅ **YoY Trend Analysis** — Price trends over multiple years using DAX
- ✅ **Sales Volume Heatmap** — Visual representation of listing density
- ✅ **10+ DAX Measures** — Avg price, total listings, price variance, and more

---

## 💡 Key Insights Derived

- Identified **top 3 car brands** with highest average resale value
- Found **optimal price range** for fastest-selling used cars
- Analyzed **mileage vs price correlation** to identify best value listings
- YoY data revealed **seasonal price fluctuations** in used car market
